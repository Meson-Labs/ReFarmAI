# PlantAI Product Overview

PlantAI is an AI-powered multi-agent system designed to rejuvenate India's plantation sector, starting with rubber plantations and expanding to tea, coffee, and spice plantations.

## Core Mission
Transform underutilized plantations (22% of rubber plantations remain untapped) through intelligent technology and community empowerment, focusing on crop diversification, mechanized integration, and rural community development.

## Key Features
- **Voice-First Farmer Interface**: Multilingual voice processing in Hindi, Tamil, Malayalam, Bengali, Kannada, Telugu with offline capabilities
- **Multi-Agent Architecture**: Specialized agents for agronomy, mechanized tapping, market intelligence, community management, and climate sustainability
- **Real-Time Intelligence**: Integration with government datasets (IMD, ICAR, Rubber Board), IoT sensors, and satellite imagery
- **Community Empowerment**: Cooperative management tools, training platforms, and participatory decision-making systems

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Rubber plantation farmers and cooperatives
- **Secondary**: Mechanized tapping service providers, agricultural extension officers
- **Future**: Tea, coffee, and spice plantation stakeholders

## Success Metrics
- 30-40% improvement in plantation yields
- 25-35% increase in farmer revenues  
- 1000+ farmers onboarded in pilot phase
- 99.5% system uptime with <3 second voice response times

## Scalability Vision
Template-based expansion to other plantation types across multiple Indian states, with community ownership models and government partnerships for sustainable growth.
